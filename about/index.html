From Humble Beginnings to a Visionary Leader

Leandro’s story is a testament to resilience, determination, and the unyielding pursuit of success. Born on February 27th, 1994, in a small village in Guerrero, Mexico, his early years were shaped by significant challenges. After the tragic loss of his older brother Guillermo, and with his father migrating to the U.S. in search of a better life, Leandro assumed responsibility early — raised by his mother, who worked the fields to provide for the family.

At eight, his family moved to the United States. He adapted to a new culture and language while excelling academically and teaching himself computer programming. At 15, a youth personal-development program and a dedicated mentor ignited his passion for growth and leadership. He graduated high school in 2012 in the top 2% of his class after a three-year radio-station internship.

Balancing 60+ hour work weeks with a rigorous course load — and his father’s deportation process — Leandro bought his first home at 21 and earned a business-administration degree in just 3.5 years. He rose to manage a $2M restaurant, then built experience across sales, operations, real estate, and a world-class company’s HR department. He met the love of his life in 2019 and is now a proud father of two daughters.

Today, with hundreds of books read, thousands of hours of audio learning, and deep connections with leaders across industries, Leandro is committed to mastering his craft and making a lasting impact in the business and personal-development world.
